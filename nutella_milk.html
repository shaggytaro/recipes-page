Nutella Milk

Description

Sweet hazelnut Nutella spread mixed with milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain or oat milk
  • 1 tsp nutella
  • 1 tsp sugar

Steps

  1. Slightly heat the milk in your saucepan.
  2. Take off from gas.
  3. Add the nutella and sugar.
  4. Mix properly so that nutella dissolves.
  5. Now put the milk back on low gas, bring to boil while stirring.
  6. Done.
