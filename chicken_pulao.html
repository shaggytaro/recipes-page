Chicken Pulao

Description

Chicken mixed with fragrant rice and coriander, served alongside yoghurt.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup washed rice
  • 1 chicken breast chopped
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 chicken cube
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • Brocoli
  • Other veggies of your choice
  • 2 tsp oil.

Steps

  1. In your pressure cooker, add oil. Fry chopped onion & garlic.
  2. Add the chicken cut into small pieces. Stir.
  3. Add the rice. Stir.
  4. Add 1 chicken cube, 1 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp chilly flake, stir.
  5. Add 1 cup broth & 1 cup water. Cook to 3 whistles. Close gas.
  6. After the steam evaporates, open lid, add brocolli & other veggies. Stir.
  7. If rice is too dry, add some more broth. Taste. If bland, add salt. Can add 1/2 - 1 tsp butter
  8. Done.
