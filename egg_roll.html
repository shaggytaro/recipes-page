Egg Roll

Description

A delectable egg omelette made with tasty spices wrapped inside a tortilla.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs beaten.
  • Add 1/4 chopped onion or 1 spring onion chopped
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper or chilly
  • 1 tortilla
  • 1 tsp oil

Steps

  1. Heat the tortilla & keep aside.
  2. In your frying pan, add oil. When it heats, pour the beaten egg mix.
  3. When the bottom side is almost done, place the tortilla on the egg. Press a bit.
  4. Now fold the tortilla-egg like a roll.
  5. Done.Have it with sriracha sauce.
